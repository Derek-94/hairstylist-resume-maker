## Who This Is For

- Fresh graduates from cosmetology school who have never written a resume
- People who used to text a photo of handwritten notes to hiring managers
- Young adults in their early 20s who don't know where to start

---

## Features

- **Survey-style input** — One question per screen, no overwhelming forms
- **Portfolio photo upload** — Show off your hairstyle work with multiple photos
- **4 design templates** — Preview with your own data, then pick your favorite
- **PDF export** — One tap saves a polished resume PDF to your device

---

## Flow

```
Name → Date of Birth → Gender → Phone
→ Profile Photo → Skills → Portfolio Photos
→ Career · Certifications · Self Introduction
→ Preview (choose template) → Save as PDF
```
